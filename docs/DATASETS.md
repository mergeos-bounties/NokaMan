# Public ESL/Learner Corpora

Publicly available English as a Second Language (ESL) and learner corpora suitable for language assessment research and rubric development.

| Corpus | Language | License | Size | Link |
|--------|----------|---------|------|------|
| Cambridge Learner Corpus | English | Research-only | 50M+ words | https://www.cambridge.org/corpus |
| EFCAMDAT (EF-Cambridge Open Language Database) | English | CC-BY-NC | 1.2M essays | https://corpus.mml.cam.ac.uk/efcamdat/ |
| TOEFL11 | English | LDC User Agreement | 11,000 essays | https://catalog.ldc.upenn.edu/LDC2014T06 |
| ICLE (International Corpus of Learner English) | English | Academic license | 3.7M words | https://www.uclouvain.be/en/research-institutes/ilc/cecl/icle |
| NICT JLE (Japanese Learner English) | Japanese -> English | Research-only | 1,300 essays | https://alaginrc.nict.go.jp/nict_jle/ |
| Lang-8 Learner Corpus | Multi-language | Research-only | 100K+ entries | https://sites.google.com/site/naistlang8corpora/ |
| CLC (Chinese Learner English Corpus) | Chinese -> English | Academic license | 1M+ words | https://www.clal.org.cn/ |
| MERLIN Corpus | German, Italian, Czech | CC-BY-NC-SA | 2,000+ texts | https://merlin-platform.eu/ |
| EF Education First Pronunciation Corpus | English | Research-only | 1M+ recordings | https://corpus.ef.com/ |
| Arabic Learner English Corpus | Arabic -> English | CC-BY | 500 essays | https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ |
